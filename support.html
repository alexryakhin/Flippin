Frequently asked questions

How do I create my first flashcard?
Tap the + button, type your text, and Flippin will translate it. Add tags to organize and revisit later.
What languages are supported?
Flippin supports 32 languages. See the full list on Languages.
How does the free tier work?
Free includes up to 40 cards and core learning features. Premium unlocks unlimited cards and advanced features.
How do I cancel Premium?
Open iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions, then cancel Flippin. You’ll keep access until the period ends.

Common issues

Translation not working

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try a shorter phrase
  • Restart the app

Text‑to‑speech not playing

  • Check device volume and Silent Mode
  • Try another card
  • Restart the app

iCloud sync issues

  • Verify iCloud is enabled for Flippin
  • Check iCloud storage
  • Give it a few minutes to complete sync
