Flippin vs Anki

Both are great for spaced repetition. The difference is how much setup you want vs how quickly you want to start.

Choose Flippin if…

  • You want a fast, polished iOS experience with minimal setup
  • You like built‑in translation and pronunciation (TTS) while creating cards
  • You want lightweight analytics that help you stay consistent

Choose Anki if…

  • You want maximum control and customization (note types, add‑ons, workflows)
  • You’re comfortable spending time tuning decks and settings
  • You want an ecosystem that’s been around for a long time

Quick summary

Anki is a power tool. Flippin is designed to be a great default for learners who want to start quickly and stick with it.
