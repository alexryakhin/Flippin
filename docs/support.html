Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first flashcard?

Tap the + button, type your text, and get automatic translation. Add notes and tags to organize your learning.

What languages are supported?

Flippin supports 18 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Croatian, and Ukrainian.

How does the free tier work?

Free users can create up to 40 cards and access basic features. Premium unlocks unlimited cards, all collections, and advanced analytics.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Yes! Enable iCloud sync in your device settings to access your flashcards on all your Apple devices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your device, or manage subscriptions in the App Store.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your learning data is stored locally and optionally synced via iCloud. We never share your personal information.

Common Issues & Solutions

Translation Not Working

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the text is not empty
  • Try refreshing the app

TTS Not Playing

  • Check device volume
  • Ensure language is supported
  • Try restarting the app

iCloud Sync Issues

  • Verify iCloud is enabled
  • Check available storage
  • Wait for sync to complete
  • Try restarting the app

Purchase Problems

  • Check App Store connection
  • Verify payment method
  • Contact Apple Support if needed
