Pronunciation with text‑to‑speech

Hear the word, repeat it out loud, and build confidence one card at a time.

A simple 20‑second loop

  1. Play the pronunciation (TTS)
  2. Repeat out loud (1–2 times)
  3. Move on quickly

This keeps practice light and frequent—ideal for busy days.

When TTS helps most

  • New vocabulary (especially when spelling misleads pronunciation)
  • Minimal pairs (words that sound similar)
  • Short phrases you want to say naturally

Pair it with good cards

Pronunciation works best when the card itself is short and clear.
