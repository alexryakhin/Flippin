Progress analytics

The goal is consistency: keep your streak, spot what’s slipping, and review the right cards at the right time.

What to look at (simple)

  • Streak: a lightweight daily commitment
  • Review patterns: when you study best (morning vs night)
  • Hard cards: what needs repetition or a better note

How to use analytics without overthinking

Use analytics to decide your next 2–5 minutes of studying. Don’t spend more time looking at charts than reviewing cards.

Make cards easier to remember

If a card keeps failing, simplify it or add a short context note. Start with:

  • Smart flashcards
  • Pronunciation practice
