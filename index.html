FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Do I need an account?
Yes. Flippin uses Sign in with Apple to set up your profile and keep your data associated with you. You can still control iCloud sync in your Apple ID settings.
Can I cancel Premium anytime?
Yes—manage and cancel your subscription in iOS App Store subscription settings.
Does it work across devices?
If you enable iCloud, your flashcards and progress can sync across your Apple devices.
Which languages are supported?
Flippin supports 32 languages. See the full list on the languages page.
How do I contact support?
Email support@flippin.app or visit the Support page.
